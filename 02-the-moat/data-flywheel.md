## Flywheel Loops

| Loop | What It Measures | Score 1 | Score 5 | Score |
|------|------------------|---------|---------|-------|
| **Correction** | Do users fix AI outputs? Is that signal captured and reused? | No capture | Automated retraining | 3/5 |
| **Preference** | Does the product learn individual / team preferences over time? | Stateless | Deep personalization | 1/5 |
| **Domain Context** | Does usage in one area improve quality in adjacent areas? | Siloed | Cross-domain transfer | 1/5 |
| **Network** | Does each new user / team make the product better for everyone? | Isolated | Strong network effects | 2/5 |

### Correction Loop - 2/5
**What you capture today:**
Learn features - AI turn on but no tracking of acceptance or feedback loop
Perform features - Star rating is tracked with option to give feedback from user but not used to train the modal. Manual checks done monthly to check for drift and feedback
All feature have a human in the loop though and can be accepted, rejected, edited
**How it compounds:**
No tracking or little feedback loops means we have no feedback on value of the features or accuracy leaving blind spots to enhancing the features and risk low adoption or quick drop of rates for new users

### Preference Loop - 1/5
**What you capture today:**
One feature does in a basic way but overall the product isn't learning from feedback data loops but this is part of the long term strategy to include this as well as learning from 25 years of learning, skills and performance data
**How it compounds:**
Risk of being copied or MCP/ chatbot technology taking over a core part of the product - professional learning

### Domain Context Loop - 1/5
**What you capture today:**
Perform and learn data doesnt learn off each other. They are seperate data sets/ products that only in the last 6-9 months have started to integrate more including the data layer to be a single product. Long term strategy is merging this data and having learning, skills, performance and personal/ company objective data will be a diffrientiator for us as most platform stop at learning and skills data and can't link it to performance or outcomes. Connected data and learning over time will move this score from a 1 to a 4
**How it compounds:**
Seen as seperate products and lose revenue opportinity from cross selling to get the true value of AI (kallidus intelligence as we call it)

### Network Loop - 2/5
**What you capture today:**
Long term strategy yes as we improve the AI to learn from others like them, team, organisational goals and aggregated data based on companies/ roles like them to give more personalised skills and develop suggestions
**How it compounds:**
Without this strategy and leveraging more user data we risk being just a generic recommender which can be achieved through chatgpt or claude
